Key Functionalities

Built For True Esports Fans

Tired of navigating dozens of slow wikis and getting spoiled. We built the calendar we always wanted.

Zero-Spoiler Shield

Can't watch the match live? No worries. All scores of completed matches are masked behind "Reveal" buttons by default to ensure absolute surprise when watching VODs.

Multidirectional Filter

Tailor the schedule in real-time. Combine filters for games (LoL, CS, Valorant, Dota, R6), active leagues, and team checkboxes. Preferences are cached locally in your browser.

Live Streams & Timezones

No more UTC calculations. All scheduled match times are automatically normalized to your browser's local timezone. Click a live match card to open an inline responsive Twitch player.

Project Story

The Story Behind eSportCal

As passionate gaming fans, we spent years jumping between liquipedia forums, HLTV, and streams just to know when our favorite teams were playing. We constantly got final scores spoiled before we could watch the replays.

We decided to build **eSportCal** as our final Portfolio Project for **Holberton School**, creating a unified, high-performance portal that keeps match results secret until you choose to see them. Over a 6-week sprint, we designed a robust architecture caching real-time API schedules locally in a private database to offer ultra-fast navigation.
